[# Privacy Policy for Interval **Last Updated: July 9, 2026** ## Overview Interval ("the App") is developed by Blue Kangaroo Packoutz ("we," "us," "our") for internal personnel tracking and labor log management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App. ## Information We Collect **Account Information:** Name, email address, and employee ID (optional) when you create an account. **Labor Log Data:** Job information, personnel names, work hours, timestamps, phase codes, location branch information, work descriptions, and completion status entered into labor sheets. **Signature Data:** Digital signatures (auto-generated or hand-drawn) captured for manager sign-off on labor sheets. **Photos:** If you select a custom company logo, the App accesses your photo library solely to let you choose an image, which is then stored for display purposes. **Usage Data:** Timestamps of when PDF documents are generated and by which user account, for record-keeping and accountability purposes. **Guest Data:** If you use Guest access, a temporary anonymous identifier is created. Guest data is not linked to a permanent identity and does not sync across devices. ## How We Use Information We use collected information solely to: - Operate core App functionality (creating, editing, and exporting labor logs) - Authenticate user accounts and secure access to your data - Generate PDF documents for business recordkeeping - Enable export via email, cloud storage, or third-party integrations you choose to use - Maintain accountability records (e.g., who generated a given document) ## Data Storage and Security Data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage services). Each user's labor sheet data is restricted so that only that user's account can access it. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. ## Data Sharing Information may be shared only in these circumstances: - **With your explicit action:** When you choose to email a PDF, upload to Proven Jobs, or save to Google Drive - **Service providers:** Firebase (Google) as our backend infrastructure provider, bound by their own data processing terms - **Legal requirements:** If required by law, regulation, or legal process ## Data Retention Labor log data is retained until you delete it via the App's "Delete" or "Delete All" functions, or until your account is deleted. Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers. ## Your Rights You may: - Access, edit, or delete your labor sheets at any time within the App - Request account deletion by contacting us below - Use Guest mode to avoid providing personal account information ## Children's Privacy This App is intended for business/workforce use by adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. ## Third-Party Services The App integrates with optional third-party services (Google Drive, Proven Jobs, email clients) only when you actively choose to use those export features. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
